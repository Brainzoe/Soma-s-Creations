About Mmesoma

Mmesoma Mercy Okezie is a talented fashion designer and cake maker with a passion for creativity. She is currently studying Mass Communications at Benue State University, where she hones her craft in both fashion and cake-making.

Her journey is a blend of elegance, creativity, and dedication, creating fashion pieces and cakes that captivate the heart.
